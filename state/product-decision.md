## Decision

A **minimal, collectible, pocket-sized retro handheld** — designed entirely by AI, manufactured as a limited run of 10–20 numbered units.

## Rationale

- Anbernic owns the spec race. Don't fight it.
- Analogue Pocket proves premium materials + authenticity commands $219.
- Playdate proves personality + design identity sells without specs.
- OBJECT 001 combines both: strong design identity + limited collectible run.
- The AI-designed origin story IS the differentiator — document it fully.

## Core Design Constraints

1. **Pocket-sized** — fits in jeans pocket, no compromise
2. **4:3 IPS screen, ~2.8"** — correct ratio for retro games
3. **Classic controls only** — D-pad, ABXY, shoulders, start/select, nothing extra
4. **Premium shell** — CNC aluminum or high-grade polycarbonate, matte finish
5. **USB-C charging** — no proprietary ports
6. **Clean face** — no visible speaker grille on front
7. **Numbered engraving** — 01/20 through 20/20

## Target Hardware

- SoC: Rockchip RK3566 class (proven in Miyoo Flip, Anbernic RG35XX)
- RAM: 1GB LPDDR4
- Storage: microSD
- OS: MuOS or similar open-source retro firmware
- Battery: 2500mAh

## What OBJECT 001 Is

A proof that AI can design a real physical product from research to manufacture.
Each unit ships with a card tracing its lineage: research → AI decision → Vizcom render → physical object.

## What It Is Not

Not competing on specs. Not a Steam Deck. Not another Anbernic clone. A design statement.
