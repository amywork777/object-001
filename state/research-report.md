- 3.5" LCD, 615 ppi, 1600×1440 resolution, Gorilla Glass
- Compatible with 2,780+ cartridges out of box; adapters for Game Gear, Neo Geo Pocket, TurboGrafx-16, Atari Lynx
- Built-in synthesizer and sequencer (DAW)
- Sells out on every drop. Waitlists. Strong secondary market.
- **Takeaway:** Premium materials + FPGA authenticity commands $219. Design-first audience will pay.

## 2. Indie/Personality — Playdate ($199, Panic Inc.)

- **Takeaway:** A device with a strong identity and story beats spec sheets. People buy the idea.

**OBJECT 001's lane:** 10–20 units. AI-designed. Numbered. A physical artifact of what AI can make when given creative freedom. Not competing on specs. Competing on story, craft, and rarity.
